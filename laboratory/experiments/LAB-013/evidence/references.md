## Physics Constraints Applied

### What the Novel Gets Right

1. **Conservation of Momentum**: All thrust requires expelled reaction mass
2. **Tsiolkovsky Equation**: Δv is limited by fuel fraction and exhaust velocity
3. **Orbital Mechanics**: Hohmann transfers, gravity assists, Lagrange points
4. **Inverse Square Law**: Solar irradiance decreases with distance
5. **Thermal Management**: Heat dissipation requires radiators in vacuum
6. **Communication Limits**: Light-speed delay for all transmissions
7. **Time Dilation**: Measurable at relativistic velocities
8. **Microgravity Effects**: Bone loss, fluid shift, muscle atrophy

### What the Novel Avoids

1. **Faster-Than-Light Travel**: Excluded (violates causality)
2. **Reactionless Drives**: Excluded (violates conservation)
3. **Instant Communication**: Excluded (light-speed limit)
4. **Artificial Gravity (non-rotational)**: Excluded (unknown physics)
5. **Perpetual Motion/Shields**: Excluded (energy required)
