## Critical Finding

**OBS-028**: Creative reproducibility ≠ Engineering reproducibility

In engineering:
- Reproducibility = consistent results
- Different practitioners should reach same conclusions

In creative:
- Reproducibility = consistent methodology
- Different practitioners may make different valid creative choices

This is a **fundamental difference** requiring domain-specific definition.
