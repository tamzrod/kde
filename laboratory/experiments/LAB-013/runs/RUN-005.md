## Theoretical Analysis

Mathematics represents the extreme of formal verification:

### Strengths
1. **Evidence quality**: Formal proofs provide perfect verification
2. **Reproducibility**: Mathematical truth is absolute and timeless
3. **Determinism**: Same proof, same result, always
4. **No ambiguity**: Formal definitions eliminate interpretation

### Potential Challenges
1. **Foundation crisis**: Gödel's incompleteness shows limits
2. **Interpretation**: Notation and presentation vary
3. **Practical applicability**: Pure math vs applied math
