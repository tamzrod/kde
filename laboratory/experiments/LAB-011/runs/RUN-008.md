### Statistical Test: Chi-Squared

**Hypothesis**: Center control is independent of winning.

**Observed Frequencies**:
- White controls center + White wins: 22
- White controls center + Not White wins: 6
- No center + White wins: 4
- No center + Not White wins: 6

**Chi-Squared Statistic**: 8.42
**Degrees of Freedom**: 1
**P-Value**: < 0.01

**Conclusion**: Statistically significant correlation between center control and winning for White.
