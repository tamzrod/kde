## Canonical Statement

Confidence thresholds are domain-dependent. Engineering domains with verifiable standards achieve HIGH confidence with fewer runs (6) than creative domains (10+ runs achieving MEDIUM).

---

## Description

The original SYN-METH-004 claimed universal thresholds (≥10 → HIGH). LAB-012 demonstrated this is incorrect.

Domain-specific thresholds:
- Engineering (Standards): 5-6 runs → HIGH
- Scientific Research: 6-8 runs → HIGH
- Software Engineering: 10 runs → MEDIUM
- Creative Writing: 10+ runs → MEDIUM
- Legal: Unknown (significant degradation)
