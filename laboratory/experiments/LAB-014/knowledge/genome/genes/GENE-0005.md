## Canonical Statement

Information DNA exists at two levels: Information DNA (artifact representation) and Knowledge DNA (extracted knowledge). This hierarchy enables different synthesis focuses.

---

## Description

The two-level hierarchy distinguishes between:
- **Information DNA**: Synthesis focused on artifact representation (20% information loss)
- **Knowledge DNA**: Synthesis focused on extracted knowledge (9% information loss)

This hierarchy enables:
- Different synthesis approaches
- Different loss profiles
- Different use cases
