## Canonical Statement

## Description

The traceability principle is the foundation of the Information DNA methodology. It ensures that every observation made during experimentation is traceable to supporting evidence, and that every piece of evidence contributes to at least one observation.

Traceability was validated across:
- Engineering (Standards): 100% coverage
