## Canonical Statement

Ambiguity identification works across all domains. Ambiguity exists in all domains and can be classified as blocking or productive.

## Description

The ability to identify ambiguity is a universal capability of the Information DNA methodology. All domains contain ambiguity, and the classification system (blocking vs productive) applies universally.

However, ambiguity resolution varies by domain:
- Engineering: Technical criteria
- Creative: Creative choice
- Legal: Legal argumentation
