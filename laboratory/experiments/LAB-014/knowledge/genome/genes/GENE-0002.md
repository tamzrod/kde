## Canonical Statement

The evidence-based nature of the Information DNA methodology is invariant. All conclusions must be evidence-backed, and this principle applies regardless of domain.

---

## Description

The evidence-based nature means that no knowledge claim can be made without supporting evidence. This principle was validated across all 6 tested domains and showed no variation in applicability.

Evidence types vary by domain:
- Engineering: RFCs, specifications
- Software: Code, documentation
- Creative: Genre conventions, decisions
- Science: Peer-reviewed publications
- Mathematics: Formal proofs
- Legal: Court rulings, statutes

But the principle of evidence-requirement is universal.
