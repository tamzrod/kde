## Canonical Statement

The Information DNA synthesis methodology has systematic weaknesses that require addressing: (1) no knowledge consolidation mechanism for large knowledge bases, (2) no mechanism to distinguish stated vs demonstrated evidence, (3) reproducibility definition varies by domain type.

---

## Description

LAB-005 (Living Knowledge Validation) identified three systematic weaknesses:

### Weakness 1: Knowledge Scaling
95+ knowledge items becomes unwieldy for manual tracking. No consolidation mechanism exists.

### Weakness 2: Evidence Distinction
No mechanism distinguishes stated claims (character dialogue) from demonstrated facts (shown events).

### Weakness 3: Reproducibility Variation
Reproducibility definition varies by domain (documented in GENE-0007).
