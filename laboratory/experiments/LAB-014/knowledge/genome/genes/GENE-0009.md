## Canonical Statement

The Information DNA methodology applies fully to formal domains (evidence verifiable, reproducibility achievable) and degrades in interpretive domains (contradictions inherent, reproducibility not achievable). This applicability boundary is stable across all tested domains.

**Partial Applicability (<60%)**:
- Legal: 50%

**Outside Applicability (Unknown)**:
- Intuitive knowledge
- Spiritual/philosophical knowledge
