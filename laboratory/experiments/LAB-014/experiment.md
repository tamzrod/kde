## Objective

Design and validate the first Information DNA Genome. The Genome shall become the cumulative representation of validated Information DNA across all KDE Laboratory experiments.

## Research Question

How should validated Information DNA be represented, accumulated, versioned, inherited, and evolved as a persistent scientific artifact?

## Background

Previous experiments (LAB-011 through LAB-013) demonstrated that Information DNA can be synthesized, validated, refined, and bounded. However, every experiment currently produces reports rather than a persistent body of validated Information DNA.

The Laboratory lacks a cumulative representation of validated Information DNA.

## Experiment Goals

1. Create a persistent representation of validated Information DNA
2. Establish Gene classification system
3. Define evolution mechanisms
4. Establish governance integration
5. Create the first Genome Version
