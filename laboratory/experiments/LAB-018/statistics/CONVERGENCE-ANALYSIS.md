## OVERVIEW

This analysis measures convergence across 30 independent Gamma synthesis runs to identify universal agreement-based communication concepts.

---

## CONVERGENCE METHODOLOGY

### Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Convergence | Agreement on a concept across multiple runs |
| Support | Number of runs mentioning a concept |
| Support % | (Supported runs / 30) × 100 |
| Confidence | Mean causal confidence across supporting runs |

### Categories

| Category | Threshold | Meaning |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **UNIVERSAL** | ≥ 70% support | Required for success |
| **CONTEXT-DEPENDENT** | 40-69% support | Situationally required |
| **EXPERIMENTAL** | < 40% support | Hypothesis only |
