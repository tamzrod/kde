## Success Thresholds

| Outcome | Criteria |
|---------|----------|
| **Excellent** | All primary metrics exceeded, quality comments |
| **Good** | Engagement rate >4%, meaningful comments |
| **Acceptable** | Engagement rate >2%, some comments |
| **Needs Work** | Below 2% engagement rate |

## Algorithm Signals (Hidden)

LinkedIn's algorithm prioritizes (per research):

1. **Early engagement** (first 60-90 minutes)
2. **Dwell time** (how long people stay on post)
3. **Comment quality** (meaningful vs. emoji-only)
4. **Saves** (indicates high-value content)
5. **Shares** (network amplification)

---

## What to Track Manually

- Comment sentiment (positive, neutral, negative)
- Technical vs. non-technical comments
- Questions asked
- Follow-up interest expressed
- Competitor mentions
