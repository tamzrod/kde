## Objective

Validate the Genome evolution process established in LAB-014. Determine whether new evidence can strengthen, refine, reduce confidence, introduce contradictions, create new genes, or retire obsolete genes without modifying historical laboratory records.

## Research Question

Can the Information DNA Genome evolve through new laboratory evidence while preserving complete experimental immutability and traceability?

## Background

LAB-014 established the first Information DNA Genome (v1.0) with:
- 9 genes (6 STABLE_CORE, 2 DOMAIN_ADAPTER, 1 EXPERIMENTAL)
- Evolution mechanisms defined
- Lifecycle states and transitions
- Immutability rules

LAB-015 validates these mechanisms through simulated evolution.

## Experiment Design

### Phase 1: Review Genome
Review evolution rules defined in LAB-014.

### Phase 2: Simulated Evidence
Create hypothetical evidence scenarios.

### Phase 3: Apply Evolution
Apply scenarios and determine effects.

### Phase 4: Stress Testing
Attempt to break the evolution model.

### Phase 5: Governance Review
Determine governance integration.
