## 10. Methodology Weaknesses

### Critical Weaknesses

| Weakness | Impact | Evidence |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **Knowledge base size** | 95 items unwieldy for manual tracking | OBS-METH-05 |
| **No consolidation guidance** | No mechanism to merge/simplify knowledge | OBS-METH-06 |
| **Character claims vs facts** | No mechanism to distinguish stated vs demonstrated | OBS-METH-07 |

### Moderate Weaknesses

| Weakness | Impact | Evidence |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **Circular reasoning** | Some resolutions are self-referential | OBS-METH-03 |
| **Power scaling** | No guidance on protagonist power levels | OBS-METH-07 |
| **Intention ambiguity** | Cannot distinguish truthfulness of characters | OBS-METH-09 |
| **World-building tension** | No guidance on intentional contradictions | OBS-METH-02 |

### Minor Weaknesses

| Weakness | Impact | Evidence |

## 11. Recommendations

**These are observations only. The methodology was not modified.**

### For Governance Consideration

1. **Knowledge Consolidation**: Consider adding guidance on when/how to consolidate knowledge items. 88 items is manageable; larger experiments may exceed human capacity.

2. **Evidence Hierarchy**: Consider distinguishing between "demonstrated facts" (directly shown) and "stated facts" (characters' claims). Current methodology treats all evidence equally.

3. **Power Scaling**: Consider adding guidance on protagonist power escalation in creative experiments.

4. **Consolidation Triggers**: Consider adding thresholds for when knowledge base should be reorganized.
