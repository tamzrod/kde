## Objective

Evaluate whether the KDE Laboratory methodology can maintain a living knowledge base while a creative world evolves over time.

## Purpose

**This is NOT a writing-quality experiment.**
**This is a methodology validation experiment.**

The objective is to discover weaknesses in the Laboratory methodology through continuous world evolution.

## Source Knowledge

Starting knowledge from LAB-004:
- World: Aethoria
- Magic System: Mana Core
- Protagonist: Young man, reborn, minor nobility
- Special Ability: Analytical Mind
- Motivation: Understanding Origin
- Threat: Dungeon Emergence
- Setting: Magical Academy

## Experiment Design

1. Continue Aethoria story for 20 chapters
2. After each chapter: update knowledge, track ambiguities, detect contradictions
3. Maintain full traceability
4. Document all failures
5. Preserve provenance

## Failure Conditions (Looking For)

- Knowledge changes without traceability
- Approved knowledge silently overwritten
- Contradictions undetected
- Provenance lost
- Observations without evidence
- Orphaned evidence
- World consistency degradation
- Unexplained knowledge
