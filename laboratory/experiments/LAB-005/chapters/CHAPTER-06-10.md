# Chapters 6-10: Escalation

---

## Chapter Summaries

### Chapter 6: The Mentor

Master Elara takes Kael as a personal student, recognizing his Analytical Mind's potential. She begins teaching him advanced mana theory, including the theory of "Deep Mana"—mana that exists below the accessible threshold.

**Key revelation**: Deep Mana was theorized 800 years ago but abandoned because no one could access it.

### Chapter 7: The First Class

Kael's first practical magical lesson. Students are taught basic mana manipulation. Kael's Analytical Mind instinctively accesses Deep Mana, causing a visible aura that alarms the instructor.

**Key revelation**: Protagonist can access Deep Mana. No one else can.

### Chapter 8: The Rival Returns

The Varen noble, now calling himself Varen Stormwind, challenges Kael publicly. The challenge involves a mana manipulation duel—something Kael has never practiced.

**Key revelation**: Duels are formal academic events with rules.

### Chapter 9: The Secret Training

Master Elara begins training Kael in secret, teaching him how to control the Deep Mana without drawing attention. She reveals that she suspected the Core Reader flaw years ago but couldn't prove it.

**Key revelation**: Master Elara's true alignment. She's been waiting for someone who could see through the methodology.

### Chapter 10: The Duel

Kael faces Varen in a formal mana duel. His Analytical Mind processes the opponent's patterns and counter-attacks with Deep Mana techniques that shouldn't exist.

**Key revelation**: Kael wins the duel but reveals enough power to attract dangerous attention.
