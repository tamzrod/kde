## Conclusion

Information DNA provides measurable advantages over existing approaches in:

1. **Evidence capture** - Required vs optional
2. **Confidence assessment** - Built-in vs absent
3. **Traceability** - Explicit vs implicit
4. **Evolution tracking** - Systematic vs absent

Information DNA is inferior in:

1. **Scalability** - Medium vs high
2. **Flexibility** - Structured vs free-form
3. **Tooling** - Specialized vs universal
4. **Learning curve** - Medium vs low

**Overall**: Information DNA is the best choice for evidence-based knowledge management where traceability, confidence, and evolution are important.
