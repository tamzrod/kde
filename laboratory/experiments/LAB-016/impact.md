## 8. Final Assessment

### Answer to Research Question

**What problems can Information DNA solve that existing approaches solve poorly or not at all?**

Information DNA excels at:
1. Evidence-based reasoning (required evidence)
2. Confidence assessment (built-in levels)
3. Traceability (explicit chains)
4. Evolution tracking (version history)
5. Systematic comparison (standard structure)

Information DNA is NOT suited for:
1. Large-scale retrieval (scalability)
2. Fuzzy matching (structure required)
3. Quick capture (learning curve)
4. Formal semantics (no inference)

### Recommended First Implementation

**APP-002: Organizational Memory**

**Rationale**:
- Highest combination of value and readiness
- Clear workflow: Knowledge → DNA → Governance
- Measurable outcomes
- Low risk
