## World Overview

Aethoria is a fantasy world where magic flows through Mana Cores that every person possesses. Periodic tears in reality release monsters called Dungeon Emergence events.

---

## World Elements

### Magic System: Mana Core

Every citizen has a Mana Core determining their magical potential. Cores can be trained and enhanced through practice.

**Source**: RUN-003

### Threat: Dungeon Emergence

Periodic tears in reality release monsters. These events create ongoing danger requiring skilled mages.

**Source**: RUN-005

### Setting: Magical Academy

The world features magical academies that train young mages. Academy entrance is a significant milestone.
