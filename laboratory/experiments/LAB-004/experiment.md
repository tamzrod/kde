## Objective

Apply the KDE Laboratory methodology to a creative domain task: "Create an original isekai world where the only known fact is that the protagonist is a young man."

**Purpose**: Validate whether the Laboratory methodology can be applied outside engineering contexts.

## Domain Modification Note

The methodology was designed for "engineering" but claims "domain independence." This experiment tests that claim.

**Constraints**:
- Do NOT modify methodology
- Do NOT add creative-specific procedures
- Observe methodology behavior in creative domain

## Hypothesis

The Laboratory methodology can be applied to creative domain work, but may encounter domain-specific challenges related to:
- Evidence types (creative vs technical)
- Reproducibility (subjective vs objective)
- Actionability (what counts as action?)
