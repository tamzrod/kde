## Research Question Summary

### RQ-001: What is an Engineering Expert?
An Engineering Expert is a first-class KDE artifact that encapsulates domain-specific knowledge, capabilities, and validation criteria for producing high-quality engineering artifacts.

### RQ-002: First-class Artifact?
YES - Experts require discoverability, lifecycle management, governance, validation, and versioning.

### RQ-003: Repository Location?
`experts/` (top-level directory) provides clear separation from Knowledge and easy discovery.

### RQ-004: Required Files?
4 core files: `expert.yaml`, `capabilities.yaml`, `knowledge-refs.yaml`, `validation.yaml`

### RQ-005: Discovery Mechanism?
Registry-based discovery through `experts/_registry.yaml`, filtered by domain and status.

### RQ-006: Lifecycle?
6 states: SYNTHESIZED → CANDIDATE → VALIDATED → REGISTERED → ACTIVE → DEPRECATED

### RQ-007: Evolution?
Experts evolve through validation cycles triggered by Knowledge updates, new evidence, or lessons learned.

### RQ-008: Knowledge References?
Experts reference Knowledge through explicit dependencies with clear ownership boundaries.
