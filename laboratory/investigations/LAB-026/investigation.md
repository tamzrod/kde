---

## Research Question

> Does the concept of a "Method" need to exist as a first-class KDE artifact?

---

## Objective

Eliminate ambiguity about the "Method" concept proposed in LAB-025.

Determine whether a Method is:
- Knowledge
- Capability
- Method (new)
- Engine behavior
- Governance
- or another existing concept

---

## Background

LAB-025 synthesized the Knowledge Governance workflow and proposed installing it as a "capability" using a "method" structure.

However, uncertainty exists about what a "Method" is and whether it should be a first-class artifact.

---

## Scope

### Included
- Compare Method with existing KDE artifacts
- Determine proper classification
- Support conclusion with evidence

### Excluded
- Designing a Method system
- Implementing anything
- Modifying repository

---

## Research Questions

1. What is a Method?
2. Is a Method different from Knowledge?
3. Is a Method different from a Capability?
4. Is a Method different from an Engine?
5. Is a Method different from Governance?
6. Can a Method evolve independently?
7. Should Methods have lifecycle and versioning?
8. Should Methods be installable?
9. Does KDE require a Method layer?
10. What problems does a Method solve that existing artifacts cannot?
