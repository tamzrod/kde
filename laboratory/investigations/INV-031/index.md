## Executive Summary

This investigation extracts and synthesizes GIS and geospatial knowledge for creating professional mapping applications.

**Research Question**: What knowledge enables the creation of professional GIS and geospatial applications regardless of technology, deployment model, or application domain?

---

## Background

Modern GIS applications are significantly more than digital maps. They provide contextual awareness, asset visualization, navigation, spatial intelligence, operational monitoring, and decision support.

Professional GIS systems must operate in a variety of environments including cloud-connected, enterprise networks, edge devices, and air-gapped industrial systems.
