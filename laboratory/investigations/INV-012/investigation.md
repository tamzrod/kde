## Research Question

> Can KDE synthesize an improved reasoning Engine from accumulated experimental evidence while preserving scientific rigor and reproducibility?

## Hypothesis

If accumulated experimental evidence reveals systematic patterns in Engine behavior, then KDE can synthesize candidate Engine designs that address identified limitations while maintaining reproducibility guarantees.

**Hypothesis Statement**: If the meta-analysis of completed experiments (INV-011) reveals specific, evidence-backed improvements to Engine methodology, then a candidate Engine can be synthesized that incorporates these improvements while maintaining compatibility with SEED-001.

## Scope

- All completed Experiments (LAB-001 through LAB-011)
