## Procedure

For each disputed claim:

1. State the original claim (LAB-022)
2. State the counterargument (LAB-023)
3. Present evidence from both sides
4. Render verdict: ACCEPT | AMEND | REJECT | INSUFFICIENT EVIDENCE
5. Provide reasoning
6. Assign confidence
