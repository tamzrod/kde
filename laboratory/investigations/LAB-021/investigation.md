## Research Question

> What is the current architecture of the Knowledge Repository and what inconsistencies, missing standards, and architectural gaps exist?

---

## Scope

### Included
- Knowledge Repository directory organization
- Document structure and metadata standards
- Category organization
- Naming conventions
- Promotion workflow
- Traceability patterns
- Cross-references

### Excluded
- Modifying the Knowledge Repository
- Implementing changes
- Laboratory architecture (covered in other investigations)

---

## Background

During previous investigations the following observations were made:
1. The Knowledge Repository appears to lack a consistent document structure
2. Investigation artifacts remain within the Laboratory
3. No standardized promotion workflow was executed
4. Knowledge promotion responsibilities may be unclear

These observations are treated as hypotheses to be validated through evidence.
